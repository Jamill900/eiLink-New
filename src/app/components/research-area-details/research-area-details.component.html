At eiLink, we specialize in providing comprehensive solutions for drilling and well interventions to our clients across various industries. Our team of experts leverages their extensive subsurface knowledge to deliver effective solutions to our clients, regardless of the complexity of the challenges they are facing.

Our drilling and well intervention services include:

  • Well Design and Planning: Our team can design and plan a well that meets our clients' unique requirements, taking into consideration the geology, reservoir characteristics, and drilling constraints. We use advanced software and simulation tools to optimize drilling performance and reduce costs.
  • Drilling Operations: We provide drilling operations management services, including project planning, drilling program development, rig site supervision, drilling performance monitoring, and cost control.
  • Well Intervention: Our team is skilled in providing well intervention services such as coil tubing, wireline, and slickline operations to optimize well performance, improve production rates, and extend well life.
  • Well Completion and Production Optimization: We provide well completion and production optimization services, including reservoir characterization, completion design, and production monitoring, to maximize well productivity and reduce operating costs.

Our team has experience working in various environments, including onshore and offshore drilling, and in different geologies, such as conventional and unconventional reservoirs. We ensure that our solutions are tailored to our clients' unique needs and requirements, and we always prioritize safety and environmental sustainability in our operations.
