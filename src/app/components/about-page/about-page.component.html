About Us

Welcome to eiLink Research and Development Center, where education links with industry for the common goal of finding practical, smart, and innovative solutions to complex technical problems.

In 2015, aiming to develop research capability in education, Khazar University and a group of professionals from Oil and Gas Industry collaboratively established eiLink R&D Center (which stands for Education to Industry Link). The initial idea behind this center was to create a mutual opportunity for Education and Industry to benefit from each other through collaborative research and scientific studies.

Today eiLink is much more than research and development centers, it is a business unit that provides value-added services addressing customers' emerging technical and technological challenges. Here, ideas, studies, and developments turn into brand-new R&D products and Innovative Solutions that have direct and tangible links to the industry and business.

Activities at eiLink R&D Center are focused on high-quality researchers, which is fundamental for the delivery of sophisticated technical solutions, services, and products driven by applicable, publishable, and commercially viable results.Their eager desire for a more nuanced understanding of fundamental and applied science is a powerful motivation for research. The compelling driver, however, is the pursuit of commercial success. To support the paradigm shift of R&D in the country, eiLink R&D Center seeks to foster an environment in which these forces can interact and reinforce one another.
