eiLink was born in collaboration with the Khazar University, so strong relationships with universities and institutions are in our DNA. To foster these collaborations, we administer a number of programs that provide students and recent graduates with resources and support for their academic and professional careers.

Our Research Assistantship programs provides students with hands-on experience in a variety of research areas, allowing them to develop their skills and knowledge while working alongside our team of experts. We believe that this type of immersive learning experience is critical for the growth and development of future researchers, and we are committed to providing our students with the resources and support they need to succeed.

In addition to providing students with a challenging and rewarding work experience, we also offer mentorship and training opportunities to help them develop their skills and build their professional networks. We believe that investing in the next generation of researchers is not only good for our company but also for the broader research community.
